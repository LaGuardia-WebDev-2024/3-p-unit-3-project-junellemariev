Custard

Ingredients Quantity
Milk 1 cup
Butter 2½ tbsp
Sugar 3 tbsp
Corn starch 2½ tbsp
Egg yolks 2
Vanilla extract 1 tsp

Time: 15 mins
Serves: 8

Step 1: Whisk eggs and sugar in a separate bowl.

Step 2: In a medium sized pot, melt butter and milk over low heat.

Step 3: Pour milk mixure into egg mixture and add starch. Mix well.

Step 4: Pour mixture back into pot, add vanilla, and boil over medium heat until thickened. Stir well to prevent burning.

Banana Bread

Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Baking Soda 1 tsp
Salt 1/4 tsp
Brown Sugar 3/4 cup
Butter ½ cup
Eggs 2
Ripe bananas 6

Time: 15 mins
Serves: 12

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350°F and lightly grease a bread pan.

Step 2: Comibine dry ingredients into a large bowl.

Step 3: In a separate bowl, mix brown sugar and butter until smooth. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well combined. Pour wet mixture into dry mixture until combined.

Step 4: Pour batter into bread pan. Bake in preheated oven for about 60 minutes. Until an inserted toothpick comes out clean.

Poor Man's Bread

Ingredients Quantity
Warm water 1½ cups
Salt 1½ tsp
Instant yeast 1 tsp
Bread flour 3 cups

Time: 15 mins
Serves: 12

Step 1: In a warm bowl, pour in water, yeast and salt, and stir to combine.

Step 2: Pour in flour and gently mix using a spoon or spatula.

Step 3: Cover with plastic wrap and let proof for 1 hour and 30 minutes.

Step 4: Lightly grease 2 bread pans.

Step 5: De-gas the dough by mixing, pulling and stretching. Sprinkle extra flour along the top of the dough and scrape sides until dough only lightly sticks

Step 6: Pour dough into one bread pan. Using the second bread pan, flip it and place on top of first pan to act as lid. Use binder clips to secure both pans together.

Step 7: Preheat oven to 400°F and set aside dough to proof for an addition of 30 minutes.

Step 8: Bake for 35 minutes at 400°F. After completed time, remove top and return to oven for additional 10 minutes. Let cool and serve.
